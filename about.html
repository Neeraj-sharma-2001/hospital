About Us

RAJASTHAN HOSPITAL

Rajasthan Hospital is a culmination of years of envisioning with the motto of creating a centre that heals the society. We are an advanced multispecialty hospital with a simple yet bold Vision – “Healing for all”. We identify ourselves foremost as a healing home and then a hospital. With modern infrastructure and state of the art amenities, we provide the best technical facilities with the comfort of a home.The hospital is designed for NABH & JCI readiness and in keeping with international benchmarking. In terms of departmental coverage, the hospital has comprehensive, clinical, medical, surgical, and critical care services and offers tertiary care health care services to the patients.
